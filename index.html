Ingredient Review

PIZZAZZ

  • If possible, avoid standard libraries
  • Customize color scheme (AND SAVE IT)
  • Color theory goes a long way!

STORY

  • Use reports over raw graphs
  • Use narratives embedded with visualizations

AUDIENCE

  • Focus on desired effect
  • Info WILL be forgotten, impact will not
  • Change thoughts, beliefs and feelings
  • Not an infodump

Idea

  • Always a message
  • Want audience to grasp and retain
  • Goal of the visualization. Supported by the actual graphs etc
